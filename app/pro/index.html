What is a .stf file?
A SnapToFile project file — your entire report (photos, annotations, company info, and notes) saved into one file you can email, back up, or keep on your computer, USB drive, or cloud storage (Dropbox, OneDrive, Google Drive). Open it anytime to pick up where you left off.
