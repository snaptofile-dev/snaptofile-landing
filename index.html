Frequently asked

The honest answers.

Is this really free? What's the catch? +
The trial is fully functional and genuinely free. The build expires roughly 6 weeks after release, at which point you'll need to download the next build (we'll email it) or upgrade to SnapToFile Pro when it launches. The purpose is to get real feedback from real field pros before we charge anyone.
Where are my photos and reports stored? +
On your device. SnapToFile is a single HTML file that runs in your browser — there's no server, no cloud account, no transmission. When you save a project, it downloads as a .stf file to your Downloads folder (or wherever you choose). Your data literally never leaves your computer.
Does it work on my iPhone / Android / iPad? +
Yes. Any device with a modern browser — Safari, Chrome, Firefox, Edge. Works offline once the HTML file is saved. The camera button opens your rear camera directly. Touch-friendly throughout.
What happens when the trial expires? +
The app will show an expiration screen and stop loading. Your existing .stf project files are unaffected — they're yours, forever. You'll have two options: download the next free trial build (we'll email when it's ready), or upgrade to SnapToFile Pro for a permanent unlock.
Can I use this for insurance carrier submissions? +
Yes. The PDF output is formatted with a proper cover page, claim info, and 2-up photo pages — readable by any carrier. We don't try to replace your estimating software; we hand over a clean PDF and whatever tool you already use for scope keeps working.
Does SnapToFile replace my estimating software? +
No — and it's not trying to. SnapToFile owns the photo documentation workflow: capture, annotate, report, export. Your estimating tool owns scope and pricing. Two different jobs. We give you a clean PDF handoff so the tools you already use for scope keep working exactly as they do today.
What happens when I lose signal on a job? +
Nothing. Once the HTML file is saved to your device, SnapToFile runs entirely in your browser with no network required. Shoot, annotate, save, export — all offline. Basements, rural properties, rooftops with zero bars: it all works the same.
Can I use my own company logo and branding on reports? +
Yes. Upload your logo once in the profile panel and it appears on every PDF cover page and photo-sheet header. Company name, address, phone, and license number save with it — set once, reuse forever. (Custom branding is a Pro feature; see the next answer for what's in the free tier.)
What does the free version cap out at? +
The free tier caps reports at 12 photos, includes a small SnapToFile footer on exported PDFs, and doesn't include the custom company header. Everything else — annotation, bulk editing, offline use, .stf save & reopen, PDF export — works identically to Pro. Pro removes the cap, the footer, and unlocks company branding.
Will my email get spammed? +
No. You'll get the download email, and then only when new builds ship or major features land — maybe once a month at peak. One-click unsubscribe, always. Your email is not sold, shared, or used for anything else.
